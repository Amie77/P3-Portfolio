Precedent Presentation

2018 Fall

Overview

Precedent presentation is a team project from SIAT’s graphic design course. The project demands students to create presentation slides about a graphic designer of their choice by using Adobe Illustrator. Our team’s topic was Kenya Hara, a Japanese graphic designer known as the art director of the Japanese retailer Muji.

Sketch

After researching Kenya Hara’s works and ideas, we planned on topics we were going to include and assigned each team member parts to cover. My role includes choosing typography and designing the layout. To conform to Hara’s idea of emptiness in design, I decided to keep the style extremely simple. I started by sketching layouts on paper.

Design Process

Since the graphics we found were all light-toned and minimalist, I later realized that I have to focus on how to maintain the consistency of texts and graphics. Therefore, I refined the design several times until they looked united in style. When designing slides, I tried to use as few words as possible on each slide because I didn’t want the text to prevail over the graphics.
